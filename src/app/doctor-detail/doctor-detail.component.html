About Dr. Vattam

Dr. Vattam received his Medical School training in Kurnool, India where he trained as a General Surgeon.

He transitioned to the United States in 2001 where he completed his Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation certification at New York Medical College and the Metropolitan Hospital Center in Harlem, New York City, NY.

Dr. Vattam relocated to Dallas, TX in 2006 where he worked as Assistant Professor for Parkland/UT Southwestern, and completed additional Fellowship training in Anesthesiology and Pain Management. He is currently board certified in both Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation and Pain Medicine by the American Board of PMR.

During the last decade, Dr. Vattam has proudly served the community of Sherman, TX and beyond. Utilizing his skills as a Pain Interventionist, Dr. Vattam is helping people suffering from pain reach their goals of becoming less dependent on pain medication and more functional in their daily lives.

Dr. Vattam enjoys spending time with his three sons, traveling and watching basketball games.
