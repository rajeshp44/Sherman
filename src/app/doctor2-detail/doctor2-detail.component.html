About Dr. Nocerini

Dr. Nocerini received his MD degree at Temple University in Philadelphia, PA. He completed his residency training in Anesthesiology and Pain Management at Parkland/UT Southwestern in Dallas, TX. He continued with additional fellowship training in Pain Management at Parkland/UT Southwestern. He is board certified in both Anesthesiology and Pain Medicine by the American Board of Anesthesiology. Prior to his training in Anesthesiology and Pain Management, he completed a residency in Emergency Medicine, became board certified, and practiced Emergency Medicine for over ten years.
